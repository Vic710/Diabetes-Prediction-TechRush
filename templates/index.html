What is the Diabetes Detection Test?

The Diabetes Detection Test consists of a series of simple questions designed to outline your current health stats, helping you to check whether you have diabetes or not and if so, it gives tips to regulate sugar levels and manage your diabetes better.

How It Works

  • You will take the assessment and provide details about your current health stats.
  • Our system will analyze your health stats and suggest the possibility of regulating blood glucose.
